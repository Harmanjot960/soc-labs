- SOC analysts detect and respond to threats
- Importance of logs and alerts

- Security monitoring
- Incident detection
- Threat response

## SOC Relevance
This directly relates to SOC work:
- Monitoring alerts
- Investigating suspicious activity
- Responding to incidents
